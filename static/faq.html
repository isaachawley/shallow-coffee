Post pictures in various locations.
Depends on Google Geocode and GeoModel and the Google Maps API

Locations! The main url is /loc/ which can be followed by /place/ or /coords/, and then a place name or lat+long coordinates.

You can enter these locations directly into the url, or move the map and search for pictures centered around the map.

/loc/place/98004/ is a zipcode location, /loc/place/Bellevue, WA works fine too.

The same thing applies to the location box in the upload form. You can enter "Boston, MA" and the pic will appear in that location.
